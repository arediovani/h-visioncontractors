The Ickenham Restaurant renovation was a complete transformation project, revitalizing the entire space from structure to finishes. The goal was to create a modern, welcoming, and highly functional environment that enhances both customer experience and staff workflow.
## Full Interior & Exterior Renovation

* Reworked the entire layout to improve flow and seating capacity
* Updated all structural elements, walls, ceilings, and surfaces
* Installed new lighting, electrical systems, and plumbing throughout
* Refreshed the exterior façade to create a strong street presence

## Dining Area

* Introduced a new seating layout for comfort and efficiency
* Installed modern lighting and decorative features
* Added durable, stylish flooring and wall finishes

## Kitchen & Food Prep Zones

* Fully upgraded kitchen equipment and ventilation systems
* Reorganized prep stations for better workflow
* Installed hygienic, easy‑to‑clean surfaces and flooring

## Bar & Service Counter

* Built a new bar area with improved storage and serving space
* Added high‑quality countertops and display shelving
* Integrated lighting to highlight the bar’s design

## Restrooms

* Completely renovated with new fixtures, tiles, and ventilation
* Improved accessibility and layout
* Added modern, easy‑maintain finishes

## Storage & Staff Areas

* Reorganized storage rooms for better space management
* Updated staff facilities, including lockers and break areas
* Improved safety and compliance features

## HVAC & Mechanical Systems

* Installed new heating, cooling, and ventilation systems
