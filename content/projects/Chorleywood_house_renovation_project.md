The Chorleywood House renovation brought new life to the property through a series of carefully executed upgrades. The work focused on improving functionality, comfort, and long‑term durability while maintaining the home’s character.

## Outside Walkway

* Installed a clean, durable walkway to enhance the home’s entrance
* Improved drainage and surface stability for year‑round use
* Selected materials that complement the exterior design

## Kitchen Parquet Flooring

* Laid high‑quality parquet flooring for a warm, elegant finish
* Ensured precise leveling and alignment for a seamless look
* Applied protective treatments for moisture and wear resistance

## Basement Boiler Room

* Renovated and organized the boiler room for improved safety and accessibility
* Updated structural elements to support mechanical systems
* Optimized layout for maintenance and future upgrades

## Floor Heating Installation

* Integrated underfloor heating for consistent, energy‑efficient warmth
* Coordinated installation across key living areas
* Ensured proper insulation and system testing for reliable performance
