The Rickmansworth renovation project focused on expanding the property and elevating its interior finishes to create a more spacious, modern, and refined home. The work combined structural improvements with high‑end materials to deliver both practicality and elegance.

## Front & Rear House Extensions

* Added new structural extensions to both the front and back of the property
* Increased overall living space while maintaining architectural harmony
* Improved natural light flow and enhanced the home’s exterior presence
* Integrated foundations, insulation, and exterior finishes for long‑term durability

## Marble Flooring Installation

* Installed premium marble flooring throughout selected interior areas
* Delivered a polished, luxurious finish that elevates the home’s aesthetic
* Ensured precise leveling, cutting, and joint alignment for a seamless look
* Applied protective sealing to enhance durability and ease of maintenance
