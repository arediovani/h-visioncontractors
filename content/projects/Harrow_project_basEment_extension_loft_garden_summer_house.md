The Harrow project involved a series of major upgrades designed to expand living space, improve functionality, and enhance the property’s outdoor environment. Each element of the renovation was carried out with precision and a focus on long‑term value.
## Basement Extension

* Expanded the basement to create additional usable living or storage space
* Reinforced structural elements to support the new layout
* Integrated proper waterproofing and ventilation for durability

## Loft Development

* Converted the loft into a functional, insulated living area
* Installed flooring, lighting, and access improvements
* Maximized natural light and headroom for a comfortable atmosphere

## Garden Enhancements

* Improved the garden layout to create a more inviting outdoor space
* Added pathways, planting areas, and functional zones
* Ensured proper drainage and long‑lasting landscaping features

## Summer House Construction

* Built a standalone summer house for leisure, work, or guest use
* Used durable materials suitable for year‑round conditions
* Completed interior finishes to match the client’s preferred style
